## Benchmarks are not operating conditions

Benchmarks are useful for comparing model capability, but production systems fail in quieter ways: stale context, malformed inputs, missing provenance, unclear confidence, and workflows that cannot recover when an answer is uncertain.

## The system around the model

Real-world AI needs a layer around the model that can retrieve context, structure outputs, validate claims, route uncertain cases, and preserve an audit trail.

## Reliability is designed

The most durable AI products treat model output as one signal inside a system. They measure quality, expose uncertainty, and make failure modes visible enough to improve.
