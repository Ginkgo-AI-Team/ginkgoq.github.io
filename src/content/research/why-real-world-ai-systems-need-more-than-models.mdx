## The benchmark gap

Model benchmarks compress the world into clean tasks. Real deployments are messy: inputs drift, context is incomplete, and users expect systems to know when they are uncertain.

## Evaluation as infrastructure

Evaluation should be part of the product loop. A system needs regression sets, human review, confidence thresholds, and clear measurements for the tasks that matter in the domain.

## System-level reliability

The model matters, but the larger system determines whether the output can be trusted. Retrieval, validation, fallback behavior, observability, and provenance are all part of the intelligence.
